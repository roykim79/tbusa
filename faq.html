faq

  1. What is Team Buffet USA
  2. We are a group that seeks out and tries different buffets rating them based on quality of food, service, price and overall dining experience.
  3. When was Team Buffet USA formed?
  4. Our first official meeting was in December of 2012.
  5. How often does the team meet?
  6. We try to meet once a month. However, some members can not wait that long and impromptu meetings are held.
  7. Are all meetings held at buffets?
  8. All official meetings are held at buffets. When impromtu meetings are held, they are not limited to buffets. We refer to these meetings as a la carte missions.
  9. A team implies competition, who does the team compete against?
  10. As a team we battle the buffet of the month chosen by one of our members. We eat until either the team stops eating or the restaurant stops cooking.
  11. How does one become a member of Team Buffet USA?
  12. Team Buffet USA is an all mens club. If considered for membership, the candidate will have a test run at one of our official meetings and the panel will decide afterwards the fate of the candidate.
  13. Are you guys for real?!?!?!
  14. You best believe that!
