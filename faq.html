FAQ

Q:

What is Team Buffet USA

A:

We are a group that seeks out and tries different buffets rating them based on quality of food, service, price and overall dining experience.

Q:

When was Team Buffet USA formed?

A:

Our first official meeting was in December of 2012.

Q:

How often does the team meet?

A:

We try to meet once a month. However, some members can not wait that long and impromptu meetings are held.

Q:

Are all meetings held at buffets?

A:

All official meetings are held at buffets. When impromtu meetings are held, they are not limited to buffets. We refer to these meetings as a la carte missions.

Q:

A team implies competition, who does the team compete against?

A:

As a team we battle the buffet of the month chosen by one of our members. We eat until either the team stops eating or the restaurant stops cooking.

Q:

How does one become a member of Team Buffet USA?

A:

Team Buffet USA is an all mens club. If considered for membership, the candidate will have a test run at one of our official meetings and the panel will decide afterwards the fate of the candidate.

Q:

Are you guys for real?!?!?!

A:

You best believe that!
